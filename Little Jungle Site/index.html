Little Jungle Resort is a mini forest park located in Cadiz City where nature lovers can relax in unspoiled beauty.

Little Jungle Mountain Resort was nominated by the Department of Tourism through the Tourism Office of Cadiz City, Negros Occidental in the ASEAN Green Resort Recognition Awards. Little Jungle Mountain Resort is the only resort-hotel that made it to the Top 10 among other tourism establishments in Western Visayas. It is owned by Jun P. Musa and Company starting year 2007 up to the present. Little Jungle Mountain Resort is a Tourism establishments that actively advocate sustainable or environment-friendly practices such as proper solid waste management, environmental awareness training plan for hotel staff, use of renewable energy sources, presence of noise control programs, and involvement of hotel staff in waste reduction, reusing and recycling items, waste separation and composting programs among other compliance standards, were recognized by the DOT and recommended for participation to this Southeast Asian search for Green Hotel establishments.
